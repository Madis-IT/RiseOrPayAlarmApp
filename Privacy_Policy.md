# Privacy Policy

Thank you for using RiseOrPayAlarmApp (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app.

## What Information We Collect

* **Personal Data:**
  We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide it, for example, when purchasing alarms feature.

* **Automatically Collected Data:**
  The app may use analytical tools (such as Supabase Analytics) to monitor usage and analyze crashes or errors. Data such as your device type, operating system, the time you interact with alarms, and general app usage statistics may be collected.

* **Ads and Payments:**
  If the app displays advertisements or includes in-app purchases, data about ad impressions, clicks, and transaction information may be collected as necessary for advertising and payment purposes.

## How We Use Your Information

* **Analytics and App Improvement:**
  Analytics data is used to better understand app usage, optimize alarm features, track engagement, and fix bugs.

* **In-App Purchases:**
  Purchase information is processed only as needed to fulfil your order and deliver premium content.

* **Advertising:**
  Advertising data is used to measure campaign effectiveness and, where relevant, present more relevant ads.

## Sharing Your Information

* **Third Party Providers:**
  We may share data with service providers (such as analytics or payment processors) only to the extent necessary to support app functionality.

* **Supabase and Others:**
  Error and analytics data collected may be shared with platforms such as Sentry according to their policies.

## Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

## Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will take effect immediately upon posting an updated policy in this repository.
